Panda English School

Panda English School is an international network with 30 years of successful training for children from 3 months and adolescents under the age of 18. More than 2 million children in 36 countries speak English in Panda English School.
In Ukraine, the network has 68 training centers in 25 cities. Students learn English as their second native language through live communication, creativity, fun tasks, games and music.
